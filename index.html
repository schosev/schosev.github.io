About Me

I was born and raised in Odessa, Texas. I moved to Kansas City with my parents after I graduated from high school. I have two sisters and a brother and they all still live in Texas. Most of my friends and my two sisters now live in the Dallas area so I generally visit there at least once a year.


Now I am married and my wife grew up in Blue Springs. We have one son and he is 4 years old. When I get the chance, I enjoy mountain biking or going for a hike.
